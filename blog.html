Rameswaram Sightseeing

5/2/2024

Rameswaram is steeped in mythological and historical significance. According to Hindu belief, this is the place where Lord Rama built a bridge (Rama Setu) across the sea to reach Lanka and rescue his wife Sita. The island is also home to one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, making it a sacred site for Shaivites. Pilgrims, spiritual seekers, architecture enthusiasts, and beach lovers all find something unique here.
